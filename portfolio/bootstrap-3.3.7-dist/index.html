Get to Know Me

"Those who want to look more youthful should live life with a young heart."

My life began on February 23, 1999 at the Sarawak General Hospital and was named Siti Arnieza. I live in a multi-ethnic state called Sarawak.

Currently, I am continuing my education at MARA University of Technology in the Information Management department for 2 and a half years. At first, I didn't know how much I was interested in this major, but after learning about it, my interest deepened. I can see that this field has great potential with a very large job market. After finishing my studies, I hope to help further my skills in this area. If given the opportunity, I will continue my studies at higher level.

Besides, this course also has enabled me to experience other professions in similar fields to my own, such as web developing. This give me chances to improves my skills in web developing subject which I truly enjoy.

I also love photography and arts. I spend a lot of my free time doing drawing, journal writing, picture editing and even photography. I enjoy what I do, and I enjoy the artistic process; it's addictive and it can send possibilities never to stop.

I am endlessly inspired by nature and also adventures, I also llove making friends with people and exploring the world. I find real happiness after I see the pleasure of others and I need to be a part of the finest joys in your life.

Think we could create something together? I would love to do so, feel free to contact me.
